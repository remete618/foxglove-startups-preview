Keep your data private and secure.

Use Foxglove to securely explore, analyze, and store your robotics data across your organization.

Identity management.

Our products use single sign-on (SSO) & Security Assertion Markup Language (SAML) for secure authentication.

Audit logs & monitors.

We track and log all events related to account access, subscriptions, and setting changes. We also monitor both our infrastructure and network traffic to detect anomalies and prevent potential threats.

Data encryption.

We enforce HTTPS on all connections, encrypt data in-transit with TLS 1.2, and encrypt data at-rest with AES 256-bit encryption.

Reliable and secure infrastructure partners.

We use Google Cloud Platform (GCP) and host services in our own secure cloud environment.

World-class data compliance and privacy.

SOC 2 Type II certification.

Our security practices and policies have been independently verified in a SOC 2 Type II audit. Contact us at support@foxglove.dev for a copy of our SOC 2 report.

General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) compliance.

We built our products from the ground up to properly handle all personal data per GDPR guidelines.

Data minimization.

We limit data collection to what is absolutely required. Any data processing will use the least amount of data necessary.

Privacy protocols.

Our priority is to keep your team's data secure - check out our Privacy Policy for more details on how we do just that.
